About Me

Developer, designer, and professional idea-haver

My Story

Hi! I'm Emma, a senior at Florida State University studying Computer Science. I'm graduating Spring 2026, and I'm so excited about what comes next!

I'm a full-stack JavaScript/TypeScript developer and designer who's shipped real products for startups and small businesses. I love the entire process - from sketching out ideas in Figma to deploying the final product and watching people use it.

My journey into tech started with a curiosity about how things work and evolved into a passion for building things that make people's lives easier (or at least more fun). Whether it's a React app, a design system, or a game prototype, I'm all in.

Beyond coding, I'm deeply involved in the FSU tech community. I'm currently serving as President of SEDS and Treasurer of DevLUp FSU. I'm also a co-founder of Logic Calendar, where I learned that building a startup is equal parts exciting and terrifying (in the best way).
